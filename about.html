Sebastian's Tortilla Recipe

Get a griddle turned on before you start. You need it hot enough to make water droplets bead up and bounce instead of evaporating. In a bowl mix:

  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • ~ 1/6 tsp kosher salt
  • ~ 3 tbsp water

Mix ingredients together by hand. The dough should not be sticky or too dry. It should be loose and pliable like fresh play-dough. Kneed it for about a minute.

Pinch off a ball about as big as a walnut round it up and flatten in your palms. press both sides into a bit of flour. Then using a floured rolling pin, roll it out to a width that is close to construction paper. Gently remove and lay it out on the griddle. It should cook ~ 20-30 seconds per side.

Once done, put tortillas in a ziplock bag and be sure to stack them, the moisture from this is critical for keeping them pliable.
